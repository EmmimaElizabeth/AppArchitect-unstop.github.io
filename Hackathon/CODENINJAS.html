FAQS

How do I create an account on your website?
Click on the sign up button at the top of our website. Enter the details and click on create account option. Your progress is saved once you create your account.

How can I contact your customer support?
You can refer our contact details are available in the contact section

What is your policy on cookies and data collection?
We use cookies and similar technologies on our website to enhance your browsing experience and collect information about how you use our site. This is to keep you signed in to our website unless you manually clear cookies in your device. We ensure that no third-parties are involved and you data is encrpted once you log in to the website.

How is my progress calculated?
Your progress is calculated based on your performance in assignments and final assignments. Through the progress statistics, you can also check specifically about strengths and weaknesses in coding.

Are there time limits in assignments?
Certainly. In your Fixed path, we have set due dates and time allotments for you to be in track with your learning. But don't worry, you can push the deadline if you cannot complete it. Please make sure that you stick to a timetable though
